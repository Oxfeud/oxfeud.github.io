Oxfeud

This was our official 'FAQ' page up until 03/12/2017 when we decided to just scrap it. Since some months beforehand it slowly became more and more outdated, but we kept it up just in case. It was first created after our first major 'scandal' involving some posts and some private messages of one of our admins to publicly codify what we do, but since then became less and less relevant. Now we are operating on a different set of policies; although the influence of this is evident, it seems that sticking to these principles is ultimately unsustainable on a third-party platform such as Facebook. As such, we it is deprecated.

Nonetheless, we've left it up here for whoever's interested. Do with it as you wish. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
